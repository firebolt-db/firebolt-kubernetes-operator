NOTE: Admission webhooks are disabled (webhook.enabled=false). The
operator runs with --enable-webhooks=false, so CRD writes go straight
through admission without the FireboltInstance defaulter or the
FireboltInstance / FireboltEngineClass / FireboltEngine validating webhooks.
Controller-side defense-in-depth still enforces the same invariants
at reconcile time:

  - FireboltEngineClass deletion-guard finalizer holds Terminating classes
    until no FireboltEngine binds (Ready=False/DeletionBlocked).
  - FireboltInstance pod-template ownership check surfaces
    {Gateway,Metadata}Ready=False/TemplateRejected with the offending
    field path.
  - FireboltEngineClass owned-field consumption gate refuses to render a
    StatefulSet when a bound class is Ready=False/OperatorOwnedFieldSet
    (engine Ready=False/FireboltEngineClassUnready).
